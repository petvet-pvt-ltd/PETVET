# Pet Owner Guide: How to Use Notifications

## What Are Notifications?

Notifications are messages from your clinic about your appointment status. When a clinic receptionist approves, declines, or cancels your appointment booking, you automatically receive a notification in your pet owner dashboard.

**No more waiting to call the clinic to find out about your booking!**

## Finding Your Notification Bell

### Location
- Open your **Pet Owner Dashboard**
- Look for a **bell icon 🔔** in the **top right corner** of your sidebar (left side of screen)
- Next to your profile menu

### How to Know You Have Messages
- If the bell has a **red badge with a number**, you have unread notifications
- The number shows how many new messages are waiting for you
- The badge disappears when you mark all as read

## Viewing Your Notifications

### Step 1: Click the Bell Icon
Click on the bell icon to open your notification panel

### Step 2: Read Your Messages
The panel opens showing:
- **Newest notifications at the top**
- Each notification has:
  - 📅 **Icon** showing the type (appointment, sitter, trainer, breeder)
  - **Title** (what happened)
  - **Message** (details about your appointment)
  - **Time** (when message was sent, like "2 hours ago")
  - **Clinic name** (which clinic sent this)

### Step 3: Interact With Notifications

#### Mark Single Notification as Read
- Click directly on a notification
- It changes from **blue background** (unread) to **white background** (read)
- Status is saved automatically

#### Mark All as Read
- Click the **"Mark All as Read"** button at top of notification panel
- All notifications immediately mark as read
- Red badge on bell disappears

#### Close the Panel
- Click the **X button** in top right of panel
- Or click anywhere outside the panel
- Panel closes and you return to your dashboard

## Understanding Your Notifications

### Appointment Approved ✅
**What you see:**
```
Title: Appointment Confirmed

Message: Appointment for [Your Pet Name] with Dr. [Vet Name] 
has been confirmed for [Date], at [Time] - [Clinic Name]
```

**What this means:** Your appointment is scheduled and confirmed! The vet is ready to see your pet.

### Appointment Declined ❌
**What you see:**
```
Title: Appointment Declined

Message: Appointment declined for [Your Pet Name]. 
Reason: [Reason from clinic] - [Clinic Name]
```

**What this means:** Your appointment request wasn't accepted. The clinic gave a reason. You may try booking at a different time or contact the clinic directly.

**Common reasons:**
- "Vet fully booked on that date"
- "Pet age incompatible with this vet"
- "Specific service not available that day"

### Appointment Cancelled ⚠️
**What you see:**
```
Title: Appointment Cancelled

Message: Your appointment for [Your Pet Name] has been cancelled. 
Reason: [Reason from clinic] - [Clinic Name]
```

**What this means:** A previously confirmed appointment was cancelled by the clinic. They provided a reason. You may need to rebook.

**Common reasons:**
- "Vet emergency"
- "Clinic closure"
- "Vet called in sick"
- "Facility issue"

## Example Scenario

### Your Experience:

**1:00 PM** - You book an appointment for Buddy's vaccination
- Appointment appears as "Pending" in your calendar

**1:15 PM** - Clinic receptionist reviews and approves
- You see **notification bell badge shows "1"**
- Click bell to open panel
- Read: "Appointment for Buddy with Dr. Sarah has been confirmed for Dec 15 at 2:30 PM"
- Click notification to mark as read
- Badge updates to show 0 unread

**3:00 PM** - You can see the appointment in your calendar as "Confirmed"
- You're all set! No more waiting to call the clinic

## What Happens If I'm Not Online?

### Notifications Don't Expire
- If you're not logged in when a notification is sent, it waits for you
- When you log back in, all waiting notifications are there
- You won't miss any important messages

### Nothing Gets Lost
- Notifications are saved in our database
- Check back days or weeks later and they're still available
- Useful for your records

## Troubleshooting

### Q: I don't see the bell icon
**A:** The bell only appears in the Pet Owner dashboard. If you're logged in as a different user type (vet, receptionist), you won't see it. Make sure you're logged in as a pet owner.

### Q: My notification shows the wrong clinic name
**A:** This is normal. Notifications record the clinic name when they're created. If clinic details changed later, the old notification keeps the original name.

### Q: The bell badge shows a number but I don't see the notification
**A:** Try refreshing the page (press F5) or wait 30 seconds for the auto-refresh. The panel will load your notifications automatically.

### Q: Can I delete notifications?
**A:** Currently, notifications are permanent. But you can mark them as read so they don't show the red badge. In future versions, you'll be able to archive old notifications.

### Q: I clicked the notification but nothing happened
**A:** That's working correctly! Clicking a notification marks it as read. The background should change from blue to white.

### Q: How long are notifications kept?
**A:** All notifications are kept permanently in your account for your records. You can refer back to them anytime.
